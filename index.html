Welcome to Logitech from Brazil


Logitech is a Swiss company focused on innovation and quality, which designs
products and experiences that are part of people's daily lives. Founded in 1981
in Lausanne, Switzerland, and rapidly expanding into Silicon Valley, Logitech
began connecting people through innovative peripherals and many computer
industry firsts, including the wireless infrared mouse, the thumb-operated
trackball, the laser mouse and much more.
